The Stance

Most instruction has it backwards.

Mainstream teaching obsesses over swing positions and perfect form. After 40 years on lesson tees, I've watched too many good golfers get buried under positions, angles, and checklists — chasing someone else's idea of a "correct" swing while their own game falls apart.

I teach the opposite. Master what happens at impact, and the ball obeys.

That's the truth I'm happy to ruffle a few feathers telling. It's the belief this whole Academy is built on.

The Philosophy

I don't teach the golf swing. I teach impact.

That simple belief is the foundation of everything I do at Pure Impact Golf. Every golfer is different, so I don't force players into a "model" swing. Instead, I focus on the moment that truly matters — the instant the club meets the ball.

I won't make a swing change unless it improves an impact condition, because when impact improves, so does ball control. My job is to help you understand why the ball does what it does, and how to make lasting improvements — not chase quick fixes.

My role is to simplify the game, strip away the unnecessary complexity, and give you a clear pathway to better golf. The mission is straightforward: help you become a better ball striker — the single biggest influence on your score.

Why The Academy

One lesson won't fix your golf.

In 40 years of coaching, I've learned a hard truth: very few golfers get meaningfully better from a single swing diagnosis. Real improvement takes time. It comes from staying on the right path — with the right information, reinforced consistently, week after week.

That's exactly what the Academy is. I keep you on track. Sometimes that's a quick comment on a swing you've posted; other times it's a full video breakdown. Either way, you're never left guessing or drifting back into old habits.
